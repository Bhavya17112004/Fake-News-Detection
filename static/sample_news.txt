Seven Iranians freed in the prisoner swap have not returned to Iran. None of the Iranians freed in the prisoner swap have returned home and could still be in the United States, it has been reported. The seven former inmates, who were released as part of a deal with the Islamic republic, did not show up to get a flight to Geneva, Switzerland, where the exchange was set to take place on Sunday. Three of the Iranians have decided to stay in the United States, ABC reported, with some moving in with their families. However it is not known where the other four are.
